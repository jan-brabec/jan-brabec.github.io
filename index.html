Hi! I'm Jan Brabec.

I am interested in bridging the gap between technological innovations and clinical practice.

I work with diffusion MRI.

I completed MD degree, Master in Science in general physics, PhD in diffusion MRI and currently I am a post-doc at John Hopkins university. I have also an industrial and medical experience. During my my free time I enojoy activities that require dedication and organization, such as long-distance motorcycling, backpacking or flying with sailplanes.
